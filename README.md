Credit Card Analytics Dashboard

Business Problem Statement

Banks manage thousands of credit card customers, making it difficult to monitor customer behavior, transaction trends, and credit risk using raw data alone. Without a centralized reporting solution, identifying revenue opportunities, understanding customer segments, and detecting potential risks such as delinquent accounts becomes challenging.

This project addresses these challenges by building an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, analyze customer spending patterns, identify high-value customers, and make informed business decisions.

Project Objectives

Analyze customer demographics and spending behavior.
Monitor key business KPIs.
Identify high-value customer segments.
Track credit utilization and delinquency trends.
Discover revenue opportunities through customer insights.
Support data-driven decision-making using interactive dashboards.
